RAISE EVERY VOICE

A VOICE CAMPAIGN FOR RURAL SANITATION

Where Words Matter Most

In the heart of India's rural villages, conversations around hygiene and sanitation are often silenced by stigma or lack of awareness. This campaign was born from a single voice—a health worker who began using audio messages to educate her community about safe sanitation practices. That voice echoed, and others followed.

LOCATION

Bihar, Uttar Pradesh & Odisha

GOAL

To generate and distribute 5,000 localized audio messages across 300 villages.

CREATED BY

Saarthi Collective

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to eliminate preventable disease through awareness. We use Murf AI to create impactful audio messages in regional languages that promote hygiene, toilet usage, menstrual health, and clean water practices. These messages are broadcast via loudspeakers, shared through WhatsApp, and played in schools and community centers. We're not just spreading information—we're sparking conversations, shifting mindsets, and empowering local voices to lead change.

Your Voice is Powerful

Every person who shares this campaign, starts their own, or simply listens and learns is a part of this movement. With your support, we can create thousands of personalized voice messages, tailored to regions and dialects, and empower community leaders to be vocal agents of change.
