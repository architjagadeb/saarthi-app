User Guide

Welcome to Saarthi! This comprehensive guide will help you navigate the platform and create impactful voice campaigns for social change.

1

Getting Started

After signing in through our secure authentication system, you'll land on your personalized dashboard. Here you can see your campaign statistics, recent activity, and quick action buttons to get started immediately.

2

Creating Your First Campaign

Click "Start a Campaign" from the sidebar or dashboard. Fill in your campaign details including a compelling title, description, target location, and relevant topic tags. Choose your distribution method (WhatsApp, loudspeakers, schools) based on your audience.

3

Adding Voice Content

Upload your voice message in MP3 or WAV format (max 10MB). Ensure clear audio quality for maximum impact. Alternatively, use our upcoming Murf AI integration to generate professional voice content from your text.

4

Review and Launch

Preview your campaign before submission. Our team reviews all campaigns within 24 hours to ensure quality and appropriateness. Once approved, your campaign goes live and starts reaching your target audience.

5

Tracking Impact

Monitor your campaign's performance through real-time analytics on your dashboard. Track listener counts, geographic reach, and engagement metrics. Use these insights to optimize future campaigns and maximize your social impact.

Pro Tips for Success

  • Keep your message clear, concise, and action-oriented
  • Use local language and cultural references for better connection
  • Include a clear call-to-action in your voice message
  • Collaborate with local community leaders for wider reach
  • Regular follow-up campaigns help maintain momentum
