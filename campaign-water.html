THE THIRST FOR CHANGE

A VOICE CAMPAIGN FOR CLEAN WATER ACCESS

A Ripple That Became a Wave

In many villages, access to safe drinking water is still a dream. Contaminated sources lead to diseases, yet the awareness around purification and storage is dangerously low. Saarthi launched this campaign after a voice message on water-borne diseases went viral in Jharkhand. That single ripple triggered water audits, filter distributions, and better storage practices in nearby districts.

LOCATION

Jharkhand, Madhya Pradesh & Assam

GOAL

To deliver 4,000 regional audio messages focusing on water safety, purification, and community action.

CREATED BY

Saarthi Water Coalition

Making Every Drop Count

Using Murf AI, we produce educational content on identifying clean water sources, low-cost filtration methods, and community-level water storage solutions — all in the local language. These messages are spread via mobile phones, village noticeboards, and temple announcements, ensuring even the remotest families receive the knowledge they need to stay safe.

You Can Turn the Tide

Your voice matters. Whether you start a campaign in your village, fund a water-purification drive, or simply share an audio message with a friend — you become part of a movement that prioritizes health, dignity, and access. Together, we can quench more than thirst — we can bring awareness, action, and change.
